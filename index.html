Interactive Galaxy Flight

Space Explorer

Cruise a starship through a glowing galaxy filled with distant stars, a bright milky way band, and scattered solar systems.

Controls

  • W/S or ↑/↓ thrust forward or brake
  • A/D or ←/→ yaw left or right
  • Q / E roll ship
  • R / F rise or dive
  • C toggle third-person camera
  • Touch pad fly on mobile
